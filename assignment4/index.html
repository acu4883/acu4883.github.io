Intro to 1/64 Diecast Car Model

Collectable Diecast Model.

Some of the greatest things come in small packages. Diecast cars are no different. Collectable Diecast carries a wide range of smaller diecast models from manufacturers like Hot Wheels, Siku Diecast and Tomica , giving the collector all the bells and whistles without all the bulk.


Whether looking for iconic diecast cars from popular movies and TV shows or collectable versions of top-of-the-line sports cars, Collectable Diecast offers 1/64 scale pickup trucks, semi-trucks and automobiles that honor the vehicle they are modeled after, but without the larger price tag. These 1/64 scale diecast cars make it easy to own the greatest automobiles ever invented, right in the palm of your hand.
